About Our Agency

25 years of certified environmental science, regulatory excellence,
and ecological stewardship across global development projects.

Core Principles

Bridging Progress & Preservation

Our operations are directed by standard environmental values and a total commitment to scientific integrity.

Our Mission

To provide developers with defensible, high-grade environmental assessments and mitigation strategies that satisfy all regulatory bodies while conserving ecological diversity.

Our Vision

To establish the global standard for green infrastructure planning, where biological studies and development blueprints work in total harmony.

Core Values

Meticulous empirical science, transparency under regulatory check, active ecological stewardship, and dedication to our clients' timelines.

Our History

25 Years of Environmental Consulting

From a boutique ecological research group to an internationally recognized environmental consulting agency.

2001 - Foundation

Establishing The Standard

Envit was established in Seattle as an independent environmental advisory team focusing on forestry baseline studies and local ecosystem monitoring loops.

2010 - Expansion

Heavy Industrial & Mining

Expanded operations to provide full regulatory permitting services for mining corporations in the Southwest and Western Canada, building certified water testing hubs.

2020 - Green Grid Era

Renewable Energy Integration

Constructed specialized ecological mapping models for massive offshore wind farms and regional solar arrays, managing bird migratory models and heat profiles.
