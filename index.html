EIA Methodology

Our Scientific Assessment Process

We follow a structured, internationally recognized, and government-approved 6-step environmental framework to secure project approvals.

01

Screening

Initial analysis to determine if an EIA is required under local legislative frameworks.

02

Scoping

Defining the boundaries, key concerns, and focus areas of the environmental study.

03

Baseline Study

Measuring biological, air, water, soil, and socio-economic conditions prior to project start.

04

Impact Analysis

Predicting the magnitude and significance of potential environmental disruptions.

05

Mitigation

Developing avoidance, minimization, and offset plans to counter negative effects.

06

Submission

Assembling the final EIA documentation for government review and public consultation.
